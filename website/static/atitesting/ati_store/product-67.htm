PN Pharmacology Online Practice 2014 Form A

Focus your Pharmacology review with immediate feedback from the PN Pharmacology Online Practice Assessment 2014 Form A.  

 

This test offers an assessment of basic comprehension and mastery of pharmacological principles and knowledge of prototype medications.                                 

 

PN Pharmacology Online Practice Assessment Details:

 

  • This 50-item user-friendly assessment offers immediate feedback for all response options, whether correct or incorrect.
  • Assessment of basic comprehension and mastery of pharmacological principles and knowledge of prototype medications.
  • Concepts assessed include: 1) basic pharmacological principles (e.g., safe medication administration across the lifespan, dosage calculation, medication error prevention, monitoring adverse effects) 2) knowledge related to the safe administration and monitoring of prototype medications that are used to treat infections, pain, and inflammation, and those that affect the blood, immune, cardiovascular, respiratory, renal, digestive, endocrine, reproductive, and nervous systems

 

 Online Practice Assessment Details:

 

  • Assessment contains rationales and alternate format test items.
  • Online practice assessments can be accessed from any Internet connection, offering you the flexibility to test yourself anytime.
  • Assessment may be accessed 5 times with each purchase.
  • No time limit for completion.

Two forms of the assessment (form A and form B) are available for purchase.
