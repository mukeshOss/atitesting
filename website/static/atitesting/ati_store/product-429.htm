Virtual-ATI® NCLEX-RN® Existing ATI User Package

Guaranteed NCLEX® Success with an innovative, self-paced, online NCLEX review course that leverages your existing ATI products (review modules, DVDs and online practice assessments).

 

  • Review directly aligns with the current NCLEX test plan.
  • Through collaboration with a virtual coach; an experienced nurse educator, students are engaged in a personalized, assessment-driven NCLEX review.
  • Online classroom provides access to a variety of on-demand resources.
  • Review is individualized as the student progresses, so special focus is given to topic areas that need more attention.
  • Review development by experienced nurse educators is independent of ATI's core program. This means that all products used within the review - including questions, books and learning activities - are all brand new, while maintaining the high ATI standards of development.
  • It works. NCLEX pass rates are nearly 100% when students follow their coach's recommendations.

 

Additional information

In this RN program:

  • Nursing graduates are assigned a coach who is a Master's degreed nurse educator with at least five years of teaching experience. The nurse educator acts as a coach to facilitate the students' progress with NCLEX preparation by providing individual support. Daily communication is available and encouraged.
  • Review development by experienced nurse educators independent of ATI's core program. This means that all products used within the review - including questions, books and learning activities - are all brand new, while maintaining the high ATI standards of development.
  • Online practice assessments including:
  • o More than 500 questions with rationales for correct and incorrect answers.
  • o Detailed diagnostic reports that are generated immediately after an individual practice assessment is taken. Students receive feedback from their coach after each assessment.
  • A Comprehensive Predictor Exam for students to take at the completion of the review.
  • On-demand classroom features with more than 250 online learning activities, links that support learning, a NCLEX Question of the Day and an online virtual bookshelf.
  • Student review materials in both print and video formats, including NCLEX TIPSTM, a printable resource guide for targeted instruction and passing strategies for NCLEX.
